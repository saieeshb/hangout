Read before you sit

  • Call ahead for the rooftop. Small room, ~10 tables inside. The roof has cover, but with today's drizzle, confirm it's open for lunch and ask them to group tables for 8.
  • Order the full spread immediately. Every review agrees the kitchen slows down at peak, so we order once and order everything.
  • Veg out of the kitchen first. Rajeev is on a clock, so tell the server while ordering.
  • PayEazy before anyone opens their UPI. 25% off capped at ₹1,000, inside the EazyDiner app at billing. Works even though Amaan made the booking.
  • Card check at the table. Anyone carrying Axis, HSBC, RBL, IndusInd, or IDFC might stack a coupon on top. Ask around before anyone pays.
  • Games stay in the bag. We are not playing Uno in a restaurant. Round two is at Saieesh's.
